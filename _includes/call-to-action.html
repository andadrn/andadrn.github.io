Find a worker near you, compare prices and set a favourable day

Taglich is a platform meant to make it easier for the clients to find a daily worker and for the workers to find new clients. Thereby, we want to create a daily working market where one can find people who do some sort of jobs or offer himself to do a job.

DEDICATED

Our solution is focused on only one thing: improve workers performances in a way that every client will be completely satisfied at the end of the day

EASY TO USE

Our app targets every generation, being really intuitive, with a focus on user experience

FRIENDLY

Our studies showed that people spend around half an hour for finding a person that suits their needs at any particular moment. That's because there is no centralised method for finding such persons. Taglich offers a friendly environment in which the time of finding a person will be reduced by at least 50%.

MINDSET

Our solution will be dedicated to our target audience. Thus we shall analyse all the needs of daily workers or people searching for daily workers and will shape our application for these criteria.
